Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)!

What is dev. Conference?

The Developer's Conference is one of the largest software development conferences in Brazil. The event connects developers, leaders, and companies through practical content, shared experiences, and networking.

Is the event geared toward beginners or onoly senior professionals?

The Developer's Conference is designed for people at every experience level. Beginners will find introductory tracks, career content, and mentoring Mid-level and senior professionals get in-depth technical tracks and real case studies Leaders and executives take part in strategic forums and business-focused content You choose your tracks based on where you are in your career.

Will Developer's Conference be in person or online?

In 2026 take place in a hybrid format. You can: Attend in person, in the cities where the event takes place Or follow along online, from wherever you are People attending in person also get access to the digital version, according to the type of ticket purchased.

Are there discounts for students, communities, or companies?

Yes! Companies get a progressive discount. Communities and groups can request special conditions. Caravans (group trips) get exclusive benefits.

Does the Conference offer a certificate of participation?

Yes! Certificates are sent as a PDF by e-mail within 15 calendar days after the event. Certificates are issued to: Paid tickets (Digital, In-Person, and Executive) Speakers, panelists, and coordinators Certificates are not issued to: Free Ticket holders, Exhibitors, Organization members.

How do I register my team or company?

After the purchase is confirmed: You receive a corporate (PJ) code Each team member completes their own registration individually on the website

How can I view my registrations?

Go to My Registrations, log in with the e-mail used at purchase, and select the desired edition.
